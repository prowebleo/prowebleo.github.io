Autonomous Data Pipelines

Three automated dashboards that scrape, store, and visualize real data daily — built as a technology showcase. The same architecture adapts to any source: e-commerce prices, search rankings, social media trends, financial data, and more.

Amazon Price Monitor

Tracks GPU prices from Amazon across multiple ASINs simultaneously. Automatically identifies the best-value option per scan with smart badges (Best Price, Top Rated, Most Reviews). Multi-series price history overlay lets you compare price trends at a glance. Built for competitive pricing intelligence and restock alerts.

SERP Rank Tracker

Monitors Google Search positions for any keyword set. Color-coded position badges (green ≤3, amber ≤10, red >10) give instant rank visibility. Area chart with interactive Brush lets you explore ranking history across any time range. Ideal for SEO monitoring, competitor tracking, and content performance analysis.

Trends Scout

Reddit trend intelligence — scrapes top posts from any subreddit daily. Engagement bar charts track upvotes vs comments over time, while upvote ratio progress bars surface quality content at a glance. Subreddit selector lets you switch between communities instantly. Built for social listening and community trend analysis.
