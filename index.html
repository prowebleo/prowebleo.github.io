Price Monitor

Multi-product e-commerce price tracking with trend analysis

E-commerce prices fluctuate constantly. Manually tracking multiple products across different ASINs is impractical. This system automates the process, scraping daily, detecting changes, and visualizing trends in a single dashboard.

Features

  • Automated daily tracking
  • Multi-product comparison
  • Historical price visualization
  • CSV export
  • Smart pricing indicators

Technical Notes

Designed around reliable scraping workflows, structured historical storage and responsive dashboard visualization for continuously changing product data.

Community trend monitoring dashboard designed to track engagement patterns, top-performing posts and activity across online communities

Features

  • Engagement and activity tracking
  • Community-based analytics
